The Legacy of Vikram Brothers

  • Before starting the journey of Vikram Brothers, Shri Kanubhai Parekh, entered into Tea Industry with a retail outlet in the year 1963 from Gujarat, India. With the increasing demands, he also entered the wholesale market in 1975. In the year 1985, He decided to expand the wholesale chain along with retail in Mumbai.

  • In the Late 80's Mr. Nilesh Parekh decided to continue the legacy ahead by developing his expertise in purchasing and testing teas. He managed to create good relations with brokers and other retailers. He decided to produce customized packets depending upon consumer demands. Mr. Nilesh Parekh's hard work, persistence and determination are responsible for the growth of the company. Today the sale of Vikram Brothers are not only restricted to Mumbai but is present all over the domestic market(India).

  • Mr. Meet Parekh, an aspiring entrepreneur, has now taken the responsibility of expanding Vikram Brothers into a new brand of "NAMO 9" Tea and reaching new heights. With this new ideas he introduced a range of Premix tea and plans on expansion of the product line by bringing new varieties of products. Today the company is not only doing the business in domestic market but also has entered the international market. He is also a certified Tea Taster.
