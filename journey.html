The Legacy of Vikram Brothers

  • Shri Kanubhai Parekh, started his journey in tea Industry with a retail outlet named Prabhat Tea Depot in Gujarat in the year 1963. With the increasing demands of tea, Shri Kanubhai Parekh entered into the wholesale market as well in 1975. In the year 1985, He decided to start his wholesale as well as his retail business in Mumbai. After Establishing the tea business in mumbai, Mr. Nilesh Parekh starting visiting the office after his college hours as a part time employee to support his father Shri Kanubhai Parekh in the late 90s.

  • Mr. Nilesh Parekh with his hardwork and persistence developed his expertise in tea testing and tea purchasing. Due to his good relations with tea brokers, he expanded his wholesale business by dealing in 5Kg of Tea packets and entered into the Packets Industry. With the increasing consumer demands, He started dealing with 250gms, 500gms, 1kg of tea packets. Mr. Nilesh Parekh was responsible for the growth of the annual turnover of the Company. He helped the company to grow its contacts. Today the sales of vikram tea are not only restricted to Mumbai but is present all over maharashtra and also in gujarat.

  • Mr. Meet Parekh, an aspiring enterpreneur, now has taken the responsibility to take Vikram Brothers to new heights. Today Vikram Brothers not only doing the business in Packet industry and wholesale industry but also entered the export business.
