Why Choose us

Welcome to Vikram Brothers, the 3rd generation in Tea Industry, where we provide you the best indian teas in local as well as in International Markets (exports). With over 48 years of existance in tea industry, we have earned the reputation of offering best qualities of Indian teas. Our team of experienced people will be with you throughout the selection process and will help you to blend the right teas to meet your requirements.

The Teas we offer

We offer a wide range of teas such as CTC, Orthodox and Green tea. We have been registered as Buyers under Tea Board of India and we buy teas from auction as well as from private estates. We make our blend unique by mixing different teas from different regions such as Assam tea, Darjeeling tea, Nilgiri tea & Sikkim tea on the merits of taste and authenticity.
