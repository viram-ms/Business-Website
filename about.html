Why Choose us ...

Welcome to Vikram Brothers, with three generations in Tea Industry we provide you the best of Indian Teas in domestic as well as in international markets (exports). With over 48 years of existence in tea industry, we have earned the reputation of offering best qualities of Indian teas. We started the business in Gujarat as retailers, after establishing the base over there we expanded into wholesale. Later we started working in Mumbai and captured market in state of Maharashtra, India. Our team of experienced people will be with you throughout the selection process and will help you to blend the right teas to meet your requirements.
