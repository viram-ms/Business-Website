Why Choose us ...

Welcome to Vikram Brothers, with three generations working in Tea Industry. Here we provide you the best of Indian Teas in domestic as well as in international markets (exports). With over 48 years of existance in tea industry, we have earned the reputation of offering best qualities of Indian teas. We started the business in Gujarat as retailers, after establishing the base over there we expaned into wholesale. Later we started working in Mumbai and captured market in state of Maharashtra, India. Our team of experienced people will be with you throughout the selection process and will help you to blend the right teas to meet your requirements.

The Teas we offer

We offer a wide range of teas such as CTC, Orthodox and Green tea. We are registered buyers under Tea Board of India. On the merits of taste and authenticity we uniquely blend our products by mixing different types of teas from different regions in India such as ...

  • Assam tea
  • Darjeeling tea
  • Nilgiri tea
  • Sikkim tea

Our expertise are in following areas:

  • Wholesaling
  • Blending
  • Testing
  • Packaging
